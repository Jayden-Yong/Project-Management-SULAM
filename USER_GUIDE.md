# UMission Platform User Guide

Welcome to **UMission**! This guide covers all the essential functions of the platform for both **Volunteers (Students)** and **Organizers (Club Admins)**.

## üéì For Volunteers (Students)

Your journey to making an impact starts here.

### 1. Dashboard (My Impact)

This is your personal homepage.

- **Stats at a Glance**: View your total **Impact Points** (Stars) earned and badges unlocked.
- **My Schedule**:
  - **Upcoming**: Events you are confirmed to attend.
  - **Pending**: Applications waiting for organizer approval.
  - **History**: A list of all your completed past missions.
- **Badges**: Unlock badges like "First Step" (1 Mission) and "Super Star" (5 Missions) as you contribute more.

### 2. Campus Feed

Find volunteering opportunities that match your interests.

- **Search**: Use the search bar to find events by name or code (e.g., "KK12").
- **Filter**: Narrow down by location (College vs Faculty) or Category (Education, Environment).
- **Bookmarks**: Click the **Heart icon** ‚ù§Ô∏è to save events to look at later.

### 3. Joining an Event

- Click on any event card to view the full details: date, time, location, tasks, and requirements.
- Click **"Join Event"** to submit your application.
- **Status Tracking**: Go to your Dashboard to check if your status is "Pending", "Approved", or "Rejected".

### 4. Language & Accessibility

- **Google Translate**: Click the **Globe Icon** üåç in the bottom right corner to translate the entire website into **English, Malay, or Chinese**.

## üèõÔ∏è For Organizers (Club Admins)

Manage your events and volunteers efficiently.

### 1. Organizer Dashboard

Your command center for managing missions.

- **Overview**: See a summary of your active events and pending volunteer applications.
- **Analytics**: Track average ratings and feedback from volunteers.

### 2. Creating Events

- Click **"Create Event"** to launch the event creation wizard.
- **Details**: Fill in Title, Description, Date/Time, and Location.
- **Quota**: Set the maximum number of volunteers needed. The system auto-closes registration once full.
- **Rich Media**: Upload an attractive banner image (drag & drop supported).

### 3. Managing Volunteers

- Click on an active event to manage its participants.
- **Review Applications**: See who wants to join.
- **Action**: Click **"Approve"** ‚úÖ or **"Reject"** ‚ùå for each applicant.
- **WhatsApp Link**: Add a Group Chat link so approved volunteers can join your communication channel immediately.

### 4. Completing Events

- Once an event is finished, mark it as **"Completed"**.
- This action automatically awards **Impact Points** to all verified volunteers.
